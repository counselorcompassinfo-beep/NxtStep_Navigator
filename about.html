About This Resource

About NXT Step Navigator

Educate • Equip • Empower

NXT Step Navigator is a student and family resource designed to make school counseling information easier to find, understand, and use. The goal is to organize helpful guidance, step-by-step directions, school-specific information, and downloadable resources in one clean, easy-to-navigate website.

Our Purpose → Important Notice

Purpose

Built to make next steps clearer.

This site is organized around the information students and families often need when planning for school, graduation, college, career pathways, and important student tasks.

Mission

To provide clear, organized, and practical information that helps students and families understand important school counseling topics and take meaningful next steps.

Vision

To create a simple, modern, and accessible hub where students and families can quickly find guidance, resources, and direction without feeling overwhelmed.

What Guides This Site

Educate. Equip. Empower.

These three ideas shape the structure and purpose of NXT Step Navigator.

E

Educate

Explain important topics in clear language so students and families understand what they are looking at and why it matters.

E

Equip

Provide guides, checklists, templates, and resource links that help users complete tasks and prepare for next steps.

E

Empower

Help students and families feel more confident asking questions, making plans, and taking action.

Site Sections

How the website is organized

Each section has a different purpose so information stays easier to manage and easier to find.

Counselor Compass General school counseling guidance and planning information. WHS Counselor Compass WHS-specific contacts, support centers, school links, and local information. NextStep How-To Step-by-step tutorials for transcripts, FAFSA, testing, applications, and more. NextStep Tours College tours, field trips, EF Tours, interest forms, and travel opportunities. Resource Library Downloadable forms, checklists, templates, guides, and document links.

Verify official information

School policies, deadlines, forms, testing requirements, and contact details may change. Users should verify time-sensitive or official information with the appropriate school, district, college, testing agency, or organization.
