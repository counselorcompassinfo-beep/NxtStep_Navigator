Simple Process

How to use a How-To guide

Each guide is designed to be easy to follow and easy to update as new forms, links, or procedures change.

1

Choose the task

Pick the guide that matches what you are trying to complete, such as transcripts, applications, FAFSA, or testing registration.

2

Follow the steps

Use checklist-style instructions to move through the process one step at a time without guessing what comes next.

3

Verify details

Use official school, college, testing, or district sources for current deadlines, requirements, and final submission rules.
